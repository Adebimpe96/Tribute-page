Profile


Dr Abiodun as popularly called is a chief Lecturer in the Department of Chemical Science, Yaba College Of Technology.
Overtime, he has proven to be one of the most respected in his department and the school as a whole with lots of achievements and accomplishments. He is a great disciplinarian and a man of his words who is willing to do all he can to see his students excel.
He is in his late 70s and have committed a greater part of his life to Teaching rather than beign a Lecturer. He demonstrates high teaching skills to make sure his students gets the concept of the course taken.

Academic Qualification


  • Bachelor Of Science (Chemistry)
  • Master of Science (Chemistry)
  • Doctor of Philosophy (Environmental Pollution Management)

Subject Taught


  • Organic chemistry
  • Applied Spectroscopy
  • Heterocyclic Compounds
  • Polyfunctional Compounds and Environmental chemistryS

My Personal Experience with Doctor


Dr Abiodun was one of the lecturers assigned to my class in my HND1 level at Yaba college of Technology, He taught the course Polyfunctional Compounds and it was a very interesting one with his unique method of teaching. I fell in love with Organic chemistry more i would say..(Chuks).
He would give us series of Tests and class assessments just to make sure we all have a good score before the Examination.
Fast forward to my HND2, he taught another course Applied Spectroscopy which was another wonderful course i loved so much as an Undergraduate.
I would never forget to mention that he was my Project supervisor too and it was really funny how i chose his name. I was scared at first thinking it might be hard working with a disciplinarian but I would say working with him was a fantastic one. I was drilled, forced to make researches and at the end was glad I had a father as my project Supervisor.
The hardwork was also rewarded beautifully and i wished to have more research works with him, and that was the height of my joy
